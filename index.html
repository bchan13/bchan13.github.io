Hello Everyone,

Welcome to our Fall semester. We have made significant progress in acquiring the technology necessary for online teaching and many of our students are currently taking lessons remotely. While we are happy to be able to serve during such a difficult time, we share the pain with those whose way of life has dramatically changed due to the circumstances surrounding COVID-19. To stay true to our original commitment to serve the community, The Pacific Community Music School has put together a plan to provide partial scholarships for the fall semester only to families in need and an additional stipend for equipment so that our students can continue to enjoy the experience of playing and making music.

Thank you very much, and have a wonderful semester.
